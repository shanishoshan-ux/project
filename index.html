Drama series

Drama focuses on in-depth character development, exploration of complex human relationships, and telling serious and believable stories (even if they take place in fictional worlds). Unlike comedy, the goal is to evoke heavier emotions in the viewer, such as empathy, sadness, anger, and excitement. .

Thriller series

Thriller series are designed to keep the viewer "on the edge of their seat" throughout the entire viewing. The goal is to create a sense of unease, anticipation, and worry about what is about to happen.

Romance series

Romance series focus on the heart and soul of the characters. The goal is to make the viewer emotionally identify with the main characters' love journey, be moved by their successes and grieve their failures. .
